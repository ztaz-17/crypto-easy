## What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that works without banks.

Instead of being controlled by a government or bank, it runs on a technology called **blockchain**.

---

## How does crypto work?

Crypto works through a network of computers that verify transactions.

Key idea:
- No central authority
- Transparent transactions
- Secure and encrypted system

---

## What is blockchain?

Blockchain is a digital ledger (record book).

Every transaction is stored in blocks and linked together.

👉 Once recorded, it cannot be changed easily.

---

## Why was crypto created?

Crypto was created to:
- Remove middlemen (banks)
- Make payments global and fast
- Give people financial control

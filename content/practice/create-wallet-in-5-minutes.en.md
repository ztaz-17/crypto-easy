## How to Create a Crypto Wallet in 5 Minutes — and Do It Safely

---

Congratulations! If you're reading this — you're ready to take the first real step into the crypto world.

You don't need to buy Bitcoin right now. You don't need to understand [blockchain](/en/glossary/#blockchain). You only need one thing: **create your first crypto [wallet](/en/glossary/#wallet)**.

It will take less time than brewing a cup of tea.

---

## What Is a Crypto Wallet in Simple Terms

A crypto wallet is a program that stores the **keys** to your coins.

Important: the coins themselves are not stored in the wallet — they live on the **[blockchain](/en/glossary/#blockchain)**. The wallet just gives you access to them.

A wallet has two keys:
- **Public key** (wallet address) — you can give this to anyone to receive money.
- **[Private key](/en/glossary/#private-key)** ([seed phrase](/en/glossary/#seed-phrase)) — this is the password to all your money. Never show it to anyone.

> Remember: **whoever holds the keys — holds the coins**.

If you keep crypto on an exchange — the exchange has the keys. If it's in your own wallet — you have the keys.

## Which Wallet to Choose: MetaMask or Trust Wallet

We'll look at the two most popular options.

| Parameter | MetaMask | Trust Wallet |
|---|---|---|
| Where it works | Browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave) and mobile app | Mobile app only (iOS/Android) |
| Best for | [DeFi](/en/glossary/#defi), [NFT](/en/glossary/#nft), web applications | Everyday transfers, storage, payments |
| Difficulty | Very low | Very low |
| Security | [Non-custodial](/en/glossary/#non-custodial-wallet) | [Non-custodial](/en/glossary/#non-custodial-wallet) |

> If you're on a computer — install **MetaMask**. If you do everything on your phone — **Trust Wallet**.

Many people use both: MetaMask for the browser, Trust Wallet as a backup on their phone.

## Installing MetaMask (Browser)

### Step 1. Go to the official website

Open **metamask.io** — only this address, no search results or ads.

Click **GET METAMASK**.

> Important: there are fake scam extensions. Install **only** from metamask.io

We'll install it on PC, choose the extension for our browser.

### Step 2. Add the extension

The browser will ask for confirmation — click «Add Extension».

After installation, a fox head icon will appear in the top right corner.

Click on it and select **«Get Started»**.

### Step 3. Create a new wallet

Click **Create a new wallet**.

You can link it to a Google/Apple account, but we'll create a password (at least 8 characters). This password protects access to the extension on your device.

After this, MetaMask will show you your **secret recovery phrase** (seed phrase).

![Seed phrase in MetaMask](/images/metamask-seed-phrase.jpg)

### Step 4. Write down your seed phrase — the most important step

MetaMask will show 12 words. **This is your money.**

What to do:
- ✏️ **Write the words on paper** with a pen.
- 📋 **Check the order** — each word must be in the correct position.
- 🗄️ Store the paper in a safe place (book, safe).

> **⚠️ DO NOT:**
> - do not store the phrase on your phone, in notes, or screenshots;
> - do not send it to anyone, even MetaMask «support»;
> - do not enter it on websites that claim to verify your wallet.

### Step 5. Confirm the phrase and start using it

MetaMask will ask you to enter a few words from the phrase to verify. Do this.

Done! Your wallet is created.

You'll see your **Account 1** with addresses on the Ethereum network (`0x...`). You can also set up Bitcoin (`bc1...`) and other networks later.

## Installing Trust Wallet (Mobile)

### Step 1. Download the official app

**iOS:** App Store — search for **Trust Wallet**
**Android:** Google Play — search for **Trust Wallet**

Developer: **Binance** (formerly). Logo — blue and white shield.

> ❗️ Check: the app should have millions of downloads and a high rating. Scammers often create fakes with similar names.

### Step 2. Create a new wallet

Open the app. Tap **«Create a new wallet»**.

Trust Wallet will offer to read the security instructions. Read them and tap «Continue».

### Step 3. Write down your seed phrase

You'll see 12 English words. Same rules as for MetaMask:

> 🖊️ Paper only. Pen only. Only you.

The app will ask you to confirm a few words — enter them.

### Step 4. Wallet is ready

After confirmation, you'll see the main screen.

Trust Wallet automatically creates a wallet on the **Binance Smart Chain (BSC)** network, but you can add others: Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, and dozens more.

Tap the ☰ icon and select «Manage Cryptocurrencies» to enable the coins you need.

## Check: What Should Be on Screen

After creating your wallet, you should see:

**MetaMask (browser):**
- Fox icon in the extensions panel;
- When clicked — a window with popular networks (Ethereum, Solana, Bitcoin, etc.);
- Your address (starts with `0x`);
- Balance: `0 ETH`, `0 SOL`, `0 BTC`, etc.;
- Buttons: «Buy» / «Swap» / «Send» / «Receive».

**Trust Wallet (phone):**
- Main screen with balance `$0.00`;
- List of coins (empty if you haven't added any);
- Buttons: «Receive» and «Send»;
- Your address can be copied by tapping «Receive».

> If all of this is there — **you own your first crypto wallet!**

## How to Verify the Wallet Works

The easiest way is to send yourself a test transfer from another wallet or ask a friend to send a few cents.

But there's a simpler way: just **copy your address** and check it on a blockchain explorer.

1. Copy your address (starts with `0x`).
2. Open **etherscan.io**.
3. Paste the address into the search.
4. You'll see your wallet's page with balance and history (empty for now).

> That page is your transparent account. Anyone can see how many coins are in it. But only the key holder knows who owns it.

## Warning: Security Above All

Congratulations, you've created a wallet! Now the most important thing — don't lose access to it.

### Security rules for beginners

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Store your seed phrase on paper in a safe place | Don't store the phrase on your phone, cloud, or screenshots |
| Make a copy of the phrase (second paper in another location) | Don't enter the phrase on websites or apps |
| Set a password on your device | Don't send the phrase via messengers (even to yourself) |
| Check the website address before connecting your wallet | Don't trust people who DM you pretending to be support |

### What happens if you lose your seed phrase

> **No one and nothing can ever restore your wallet.**

Not MetaMask, not Trust Wallet, not the developers. The technology is designed so that the phrase is the only key. Lose it — say goodbye to your money.

That's why:
- the seed phrase is written by hand;
- stored in two places;
- verified that all 12 words are correct.

But most importantly — now you have a **tool** that gives you full control over your money. No banks, no regulators, no one else can freeze or [block](/en/glossary/#block) the funds in your wallet.

> You hold the keys. You control your money.

## FAQ

### Can I create multiple wallets?
Yes. One MetaMask can manage multiple accounts. In Trust Wallet — just create a new wallet (or import another).

### What is a «hot wallet»?
MetaMask and Trust Wallet are **hot wallets** ([hot wallet](/en/glossary/#hot-wallet)). The seed phrase is protected by software, but if your PC gets infected, the keys could be compromised.

For large amounts, use **cold wallets** ([cold wallet](/en/glossary/#cold-wallet)).
> A cold wallet is an isolated device that stores your seed phrase inside, signs transactions, and outputs the result to your PC/phone via Bluetooth or USB. Viruses are practically unable to hack a cold wallet because it's a separate encrypted device (a mini-PC).

### Is it safe to keep all my money in MetaMask?
For small amounts (up to $500–1000) — yes. For serious savings, better get a hardware wallet (Ledger, Trezor).

### Can I restore my wallet on another device?
Yes, if you have your seed phrase. Just install MetaMask or Trust Wallet and select «Import Wallet».

### What if my seed phrase is leaked?
Immediately create a new wallet and transfer all funds from the old one. The old wallet can no longer be used.

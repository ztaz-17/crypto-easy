## Privacy Policy

### What data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transfer your personal data. This site is static — we have no server, registration, or data input forms.

### Analytics

For visit statistics, we use:

- **Google Analytics (GA4)** — anonymous visit data (country, browser, device). IP addresses are anonymized. [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Yandex.Metrica** — anonymous visit statistics. [Yandex's Privacy Policy](https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/)

You can disable tracking through browser settings or ad blockers.

### Cookies

We do not use our own cookies. Third-party services (Google Analytics, Yandex.Metrica) may set cookies for analytics.

### Third-party services

The site may contain links to external resources (exchanges, wallets, Telegram). We are not responsible for their privacy policies.

If you have questions — [write to us](/en/contacts/).
